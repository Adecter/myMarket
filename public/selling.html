Furniture value estimator

Furniture will be evaluated by the best specialists in our industry.

After submitting an application for sale, your application will be considered within 7 business days and after this time you will be sent a report with the proposed price of your furniture.

You can also open a dispute and we will attach additional information. Within 5 working days we will consider your dispute and an independent commission will be invited. If after an independent commission you are not satisfied with the price of your furniture, you have the opportunity to visit one of our branches to discuss the final price.

Mortgage center

Also, you have the opportunity, with the help of our company, to partially repay a mortgage loan.

To do this, you need to fill out a form in one of our branches and within 7 working days our employees will call you back to visit and evaluate your furniture.

Cash payments will be made 30 business days after the confirmation and evaluation of your application. Our company transports all the furniture, so you don’t have to worry about transporting furniture to the warehouse.
